About MBA

Empowering diverse voices in business since 1998

Who We Are

The Multicultural Business Association (MBA) at Columbia University is a dynamic student organization dedicated to fostering diversity, inclusion, and professional development in the business world. Founded in 1998, we have been at the forefront of creating opportunities for underrepresented students in business and finance.

Our organization brings together students from diverse cultural, ethnic, and socioeconomic backgrounds who share a passion for business and a commitment to making a positive impact in their communities. We believe that diversity drives innovation and that inclusive business practices lead to better outcomes for everyone.

Our Mission

Empowering Underrepresented Voices

We are committed to providing underrepresented students with the resources, connections, and opportunities they need to succeed in the competitive world of business and finance. Through mentorship, networking, and professional development programs, we help our members build the skills and confidence necessary for long-term success.

Building Inclusive Networks

We create spaces where students from all backgrounds can connect, collaborate, and learn from each other. Our diverse membership brings together unique perspectives and experiences, fostering an environment of mutual respect and understanding that prepares our members for the global business landscape.

Driving Business Innovation

By promoting diversity and inclusion in business, we contribute to the development of more innovative, effective, and socially responsible business practices. Our members go on to become leaders who champion these values in their professional careers and communities.
